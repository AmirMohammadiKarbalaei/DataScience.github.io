About me

I am a dedicated data scientist with a passion for uncovering meaningful insights from complex data.

In the field of coding and data science, I am proficient in Python (and related libraries) as well as SQL. I also specialise in creating informative visualisations using Power BI and Tableau. My projects cover a wide range of topics, from predictive analytics to deep learning demonstrating my expertise across diverse areas. Beyond technical skills, my primary focus is on using data to drive positive societal change. I believe that uncovering insights is not just a task; it's a commitment to guiding decisions for the betterment of society.

Looking ahead, I am deeply invested in advancing a future where data science is ethical, transparent, and inclusive. By championing responsible AI practices and fostering interpretability, I strive to contribute to a field that balances innovation with accountability.
